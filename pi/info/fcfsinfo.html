First Come First Serve

In the FCFS scheduling, tasks are implemented on first come, first serve basis. It can be a non-preemptive as well as preemptive scheduling algorithm based on the necessities. Jobs are processed in the order that they arrive

  • Advantages
      • Simple to schedule.
      Easy to implement;
      Every process is executed hence no issue of starvation.
      No CPU overhead for schedling point calculations.
  • Disadvantages
      • No Option for priority and preemption and hence deadline miss may occure in case of deadline constarints.
      CPU task waiting time becomes large when large process is executed.
