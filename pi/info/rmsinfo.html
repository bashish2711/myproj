Rate Monotonic Scheduling

RMS is static and preemptive scheduling. The priority is assigned according to period, where period is equal to deadline. The tasks with shorter period will be given highest priority.

  • Advantages
      • Best Suited for periodic Tasks
      RMS can be used upto 77% utilization bound
      Simple to use.
  • Disadvantages
      • Not all real time tasks are periodic and if the period is not known these policies can not be used.
      In case the utilization upper bound of RMS is not satisfied the predictablity is not sure.
